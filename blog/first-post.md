Welcome to **AI in Motion: Foundations of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**! In this post, we will guide you through setting up your first humanoid robot simulation using ROS 2 and Gazebo.

**Steps to get started:**
1. Ensure your development environment is ready (ROS 2, Gazebo, Python).
2. Open Module 1 tutorials in the Docs section to understand basic ROS 2 nodes and topics.
3. Run the provided simulation scripts to see your humanoid robot move.

This blog will help you get hands-on experience and complement the tutorials. Screenshots, visual guides, and tips will follow in upcoming posts. Explore, experiment, and enjoy building your first robot!
